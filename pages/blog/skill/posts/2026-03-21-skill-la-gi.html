SKILL la gi va cach thiet ke SKILL de tang toc do lam viec

Topic: SKILL Author: Huynh Thanh Xuan 7 min read

Cach dong goi kien thuc va quy trinh thanh SKILL de tai su dung, giam lap lai khi lam viec voi AI agent.

1. Why this topic matters

Khi du an lon dan, viec lap lai huong dan va quy trinh gay ton thoi gian.

SKILL giup chuan hoa cach lam va tang toc do onboarding.

2. Core idea

SKILL la mot goi huong dan + tai nguyen + script theo mot bai toan cu the.

Thay vi moi lan viet lai prompt, ta tai su dung SKILL nhu mot module.

3. Step-by-step breakdown

  1. Xac dinh bai toan lap lai nhieu lan.
  2. Trich xuat checklist, template, script can thiet.
  3. Dong goi thanh SKILL va cap nhat theo vong feedback.

4. Example

SKILL cho code review co the gom: checklist bug risk, mau comment, script scan nhanh va tieu chi severity.

5. Common mistakes

  • SKILL qua tong quat nen kho dung.
  • Khong co vi du dau vao/ra cu the.
  • Khong cap nhat khi context du an thay doi.

6. Key takeaways

  • SKILL hieu qua nhat khi co pham vi hep va ro.
  • Nen co owner va lich review dinh ky cho tung SKILL.
  • SKILL tot giup team dong deu chat luong dau ra.
