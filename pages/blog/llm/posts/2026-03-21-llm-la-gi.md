## Why this topic matters

LLM dang tro thanh lop nang luc co ban cho nhieu san pham phan mem moi. Hieu ro LLM giup chung ta dua ra quyet dinh ky thuat dung ngay tu dau.

## Core idea

LLM la mo hinh ngon ngu lon duoc huan luyen tren luong du lieu van ban rat lon de du doan token tiep theo. Tu co che nay, mo hinh co the tom tat, hoi dap, viet noi dung va ho tro lap trinh.

## Step-by-step breakdown

1. Xac dinh bai toan nghiep vu can LLM ho tro.
2. Chon model phu hop theo chat luong, toc do, chi phi.
3. Thiet ke luong input-output ro rang va co cach danh gia.

## Example

Vi du chatbot ho tro khach hang: thu thap cau hoi pho bien, tao prompt khung, them context tu kho tri thuc noi bo, sau do do luong do chinh xac va ti le can thiep cua nguoi.

## Common mistakes

- Chon model theo trend ma khong benchmark.
- Thieu bo test case dai dien cho du lieu that.
- Khong theo doi chi phi token khi scale.

## Key takeaways

- LLM manh nhung can architecture dung.
- Prompt, context va eval quan trong ngang model.
- Bat dau nho, do luong ky, roi moi mo rong.
