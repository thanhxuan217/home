Open Claw va Agentic AI: tu chatbot den he thong tu hanh dong

Topic: Open Claw va Agentic AI Author: Huynh Thanh Xuan 9 min read

Goc nhin tong quan ve Open Claw va Agentic AI, tap trung vao kieu kien truc va cac luu y khi dua vao production.

1. Why this topic matters

Agentic AI dang mo ra cach xay dung phan mem moi: he thong khong chi tra loi ma con lap ke hoach va thuc thi tac vu.

2. Core idea

Agentic AI thuong gom planner, memory, tool-use va evaluator.

Open Claw co the duoc nhin nhu mot huong tiep can de to chuc cac thanh phan nay thanh luong xu ly co kiem soat.

3. Step-by-step breakdown

  1. Chia nho muc tieu thanh cac tac vu co the do luong.
  2. Gan tool phu hop cho tung tac vu va dat guardrail.
  3. Them loop danh gia-decision de giam loi va tang on dinh.

4. Example

Agent xu ly bug report: doc issue, tai hien loi, de xuat patch, chay test, tao pull request nhap mo ta thay doi.

5. Common mistakes

  • Cho agent quyen qua rong ngay tu dau.
  • Thieu logging va observability cho tung buoc.
  • Khong xac dinh dieu kien dung de tranh vong lap.

6. Key takeaways

  • Agentic AI manh khi duoc gioi han scope ro rang.
  • Tooling, guardrail va eval la ba cot tru cho production.
  • Nen trien khai theo tung lop tu ban tu dong nho den luong phuc tap.
